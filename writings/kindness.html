i am down on my knees
i have been tying my shoes for years
and i cannot rise until i am finished

my hands begin to tremble each time i am almost close
but when i look up to apologize
your eyes are still understanding

you've always had steady eyes
you place your hand upon my shoulder

you've always had steady hands


i hate your legs
those great muscles that have never crumpled beneath you or have ever failed to rise

i am ashamed i could even think such a thing
