waiting for you is
waiting for black holes to die
and for all of this half of time
to come to its climax

but the ice melts so often in my hand, since I am still so very alive
.

people say bless you as I pass them on the street,
because it is summer


they are playing bridge when I arrive,
I do not think they will play bridge with me

ambitiously, I bite to the center of my apple,
only to find that it is hollow;
removed of all its seeds


the numbers have grown too big to make a decision,

and I am no tuesday morning mathematician
