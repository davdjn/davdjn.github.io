I
she cried and cried for days
after they cut him from her

and now her room is empty,
cleaned of everything it once needed


II
for even all the saviors of all those
passing strangers

yours is a face too familiar
to ever be seen like this

this is a place too far
to ever reconcile the distance


III
when the child is to be weaned,
the mother understands her blindness.
she does not look back at limping, and she will never lose her child.

how fortunate for the mother who can let go so easily!
