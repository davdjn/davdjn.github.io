only disappointment understands perfection


when the shadow of the evening is hidden by the light of the midday sun

and the man with the second-most

quietly weeps

~

the sky was overcast

the day he was born

and he would never be happy.

they named him taro

like the root vegetable

~~

some people sleep during a train ride

when they know where they are going

and they know when they will get there.


they are happy to find a decently-sized flowerpot to plant themselves in
